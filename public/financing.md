# Smooth Fence USA — Financing

> Your new fence, with monthly payments. A $5,000 project can be as low as
> ~$417/month. Multiple financing options available.

## How it works

1. **Choose your fence.** Use the [Instant Estimator](/estimator) or
   schedule a free visit to get your quote.
2. **Select financing.** Pick the option that fits your budget. Prequalify
   in minutes — no impact on your credit score with Hearth.
3. **Enjoy your fence.** We install your fence and you pay comfortably in
   monthly installments.

## Financing partners

### GetHearth (Recommended) — Home Improvement Financing

Purpose-built for fence and home improvement projects. Long-term financing
up to 12 years with competitive APR. Prequalify with a soft credit check —
no impact to your score.

- Up to $100K
- Terms 2–12 years
- Soft credit check
- Apply in 2 minutes

[Apply with GetHearth →](/financing) (link opens GetHearth in a new tab via
the merchant page)

### Affirm — Pay Over Time (deposits)

Split your deposit or full project cost into fixed monthly payments. Choose
3, 6, or 12-month plans. Available at checkout when you pay online.

- 3–12 months
- Fixed payments
- At checkout
- Quick approval

### Afterpay — Buy Now, Pay Later (deposits)

Split your payment into 4 interest-free installments over 6 weeks. Great
for deposits and smaller payments. No interest, no fees when you pay on
time.

- 4 payments
- 0% interest
- 6 weeks
- No fees

### Klarna — Flexible Payments (deposits)

Pay in 4 interest-free installments, or finance over 6–36 months. Smooth
checkout experience with instant approval.

- 4 installments or 6–36 months
- Instant approval
- Flexible
