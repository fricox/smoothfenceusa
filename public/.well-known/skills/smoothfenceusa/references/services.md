# Smooth Fence USA — Services Reference

Detail per service type. Companion to SKILL.md.

## Vinyl

- Material: virgin (non-recycled) vinyl.
- Styles: full privacy, privacy with decorative top, semi-privacy, picket.
- Mounting: routed (rails inserted directly into posts, cleaner look) or
  bracket-mounted (external connectors).
- Heights: standard residential heights; HOA-driven sizing on request.
- Strengths: low maintenance, stays bright, performs well in Florida
  coastal climate.
- Manufacturer warranty: typically limited lifetime.

## Aluminum

- Won't rust. Holds up to Florida heat, humidity, salt air.
- Common applications: pool fencing, front yards, HOA communities where
  the look needs to be open + clean.
- Pool-code compliant hardware available on request: self-closing,
  self-latching, latch ≥54 inches above grade, gates open outward from
  the pool.
- Manufacturer warranty: typically 10–20 years.

## Chain-link

- Cost-effective option for property security, pets, work areas.
- Pros: visibility, durability, lower cost than vinyl/aluminum/wood.
- Cons: less privacy.
- Manufacturer warranty: varies by product.

## Wood

- Classic look. Performs well with proper installation (modern hardware
  + correct post setting).
- Risk without proper install: leaning posts and sagging after first
  storm. SFUSA setting practice avoids this.
- Manufacturer warranty: varies by product.

## Gates

- Standard walk-gates: 4 ft and 5 ft wide.
- Custom: any opening, any material; additional charges for fabrication,
  installation, transportation.
- Hardware: heavy-duty galvanized or stainless-steel, no brand commitment
  (preferences accepted).
- Manual only — no automation, no keypads, no card-readers, no
  intercoms.

## Fence repair & storm damage

- Repairs leaning panels, broken posts, gate alignment issues, damaged
  sections.
- For storm-damaged fences: SFUSA inspects and decides repair vs full
  replacement on a case-by-case basis.
- Partial repair available when damage is localized; widespread structural
  issues usually point to replacement.

## HOA & permits assistance

- Review HOA rules, propose compliant style/material/color.
- Provide drawings or descriptions to support the HOA application
  (homeowner submits).
- Identify projects that require city/county permits.
- Coordinate permit steps when applicable.

## What we don't offer

- Commercial fencing (residential only).
- Gate automation, keypads, card readers, intercoms.
- Specific hardware brand commitments.
- Pricing without on-site visit.
- SLA promises beyond the written warranty.
