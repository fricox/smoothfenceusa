# Smooth Fence USA — Process Reference

Estimate → measure → quote → deposit → install. Companion to SKILL.md.

## Step 1 — Initial contact

Channels:
- Web form at /contact
- Instant estimator at /estimator (preferred — fastest)
- Phone (386) 403-9460
- SMS (386) 403-9460
- Email info@smoothfenceusa.com
- Calendly at calendly.com/federico-smoothfenceusa/30min

The instant estimator captures the project scope (material, linear feet,
gates, removal) and the homeowner contact info, and emails the homeowner
a price range immediately.

## Step 2 — Quick call

We confirm:
- Project scope
- HOA status (yes/no/not sure)
- Preferred installation timeline
- Special site conditions (pool, slope, easements, etc.)

## Step 3 — Free on-site visit

Within 24 hours of confirming.

The estimator (often the owner) measures:
- Exact linear feet
- Grading and slope
- Utility locations (overhead and underground markings)
- Property lines (note: final determination comes from a survey)
- Gate locations
- Old fence to remove (if any)

## Step 4 — Written proposal

Includes:
- Materials and quantities
- Style, height, color
- Hardware specification
- Gate count and sizing
- Permits/HOA support items
- Installation timeline
- Total price
- Deposit amount (50%) and payment terms

## Step 5 — Deposit

50% deposit via Stripe checkout (/pay).

Affirm, Afterpay, and Klarna are available at deposit checkout for
homeowners who prefer to split the deposit.

Hearth financing is available for the full project (apply on /financing).

## Step 6 — HOA / permits

If needed:
- Homeowner submits the HOA application using the drawings/specs SFUSA
  provided.
- SFUSA coordinates permit steps when applicable.

Wait time depends on the HOA and jurisdiction.

## Step 7 — Installation

Typical residential install: 1–3 days.

Factors affecting duration:
- Project size (linear feet)
- Terrain
- Weather
- Site access
- Material delivery timing

Homeowner preparation before install day:
- Clear property access
- Clear the work area
- Secure pets
- HOA/permits underway if applicable

## Step 8 — Balance + warranty

Balance paid on completion.

Warranty documentation delivered with the project:
- 5-year workmanship warranty (non-transferable)
- Manufacturer warranties per material

## Step 9 — Aftercare

Same-day response to warranty questions during business hours
(Mon–Sat, 8 AM – 6 PM).

Warranty claims:
- Call (386) 403-9460 or email info@smoothfenceusa.com
- Include photos and description
- Federico schedules an inspection
