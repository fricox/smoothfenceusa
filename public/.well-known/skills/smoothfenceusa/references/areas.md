# Smooth Fence USA — Service Areas Reference

Five Northeast Florida counties. Detail per county. Companion to SKILL.md.

## Flagler County (HQ)

- Key cities: Palm Coast, Bunnell, Flagler Beach.
- Hero city: Palm Coast.
- HQ location. Fastest response time.

## Volusia County

## St. Johns County

## Duval County

## Putnam County

## Geographic factors that affect quotes

- Distance from Palm Coast HQ (transport time).
- Terrain (flat vs sloped, sandy vs rocky).
- Coastal exposure (salt air, recommended materials).
- HOA density of the neighborhood.
- Permit jurisdiction (city vs unincorporated county).
