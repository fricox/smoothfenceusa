## Quote process

1. Instant estimator at https://www.smoothfenceusa.com/estimator — answer
   6 questions, get a price range in ~30 seconds.
2. Free on-site visit within 24 hours — the owner or estimator measures
   linear feet, checks grading, utilities, property lines, and HOA
   requirements.
3. Written proposal — detailed quote with materials, permits, scheduling.
   No hidden fees.
4. 50% deposit (via Stripe checkout on /pay) to lock the schedule.
5. Balance on completion.

Pricing factors: material, height, linear footage, gate count, terrain,
layout, old-fence removal, HOA/permit complexity. Firm pricing only after
the free on-site visit.

Pricing tier: `$$` (mid-market for the region). Example: a $5,000 project
can be financed at ~$417/month through Hearth.

## HOA & permits

Many neighborhoods in Northeast Florida are part of an HOA with specific
rules (height, style, material, color). Some projects also require city
or county permits.

Smooth Fence USA helps clients:
- Review HOA rules & design guidelines
- Choose styles/colors that fit HOA approval
- Provide drawings/descriptions for HOA application
- Identify which projects require permits
- Coordinate permit steps when applicable
- Plan layouts respecting property lines and easements

The homeowner typically submits the HOA application; Smooth Fence USA
provides the documentation. Final boundary determinations come from a
survey or the proper authority.

## Warranty

- **5-year workmanship warranty** on every residential installation.
  Covers: panels loosening from improper fastening, posts failing due to
  incorrect setting, gates misaligned at install, similar
  installation-related defects.
- **Manufacturer warranties:** vinyl typically limited lifetime; aluminum
  typically 10–20 years; wood and chain-link vary by product.
- **NOT covered:** storm damage, hurricanes, flooding, vehicle/tree
  impact, vandalism, lack of maintenance, normal wear, sun-fade
  discoloration, third-party modifications after installation.
- Workmanship warranty is non-transferable (original homeowner only).
- Full terms in the written contract delivered with the project.
- Claims: call (386) 403-9460 or email info@smoothfenceusa.com with
  photos + description. Federico will schedule an inspection.

## How to request a quote (agent guidance)

When a user asks you to request a quote, gather these details and direct
them to the contact form at https://www.smoothfenceusa.com/contact, or to
the instant estimator at https://www.smoothfenceusa.com/estimator:

**Required information:**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Property address (full street address)
- Fence type interest (vinyl / aluminum / chain-link / wood / not sure)
- Approximate linear feet (rough estimate is fine)
- HOA involved? (yes / no / not sure)
- Preferred visit date

**Optional information:**
- Specific style preference (privacy, semi-privacy, picket, decorative top)
- Number of gates
- Pool gate required (triggers pool-code-compliant hardware)
- Old fence removal needed
- Financing of interest

## Constraints (what NOT to promise)

- **No firm pricing without an on-site visit.** The estimator gives a
  range; the visit confirms exact.
- **No specific install timeline beyond "1–3 days typical".**
- **No gate automation, no keypad/intercom/card-reader access control.**
  Manual gates only.
- **No specific hardware brand commitment.** Heavy-duty galvanized or
  stainless-steel, sized per project; brand preferences accepted.
- **No commercial fencing.** Residential only.
- **No SLA promises beyond what's in the written warranty.**
