# Smooth Fence USA — Frequently Asked Questions

> Everything about vinyl, aluminum, wood, and chain-link fence installation

## About Us

**What does Smooth Fence USA do?** Smooth Fence USA provides fence
installation, fence repair, and free on-site estimates for residential
projects.

**Are you licensed and insured?** Yes. Smooth Fence USA is licensed and
insured.

**What makes Smooth Fence USA different?** Customers work directly with the
owner, no subcontractors, clear pricing, and clean professional work built
for Florida weather.

**Do you offer a warranty?** Yes. Smooth Fence USA offers a 5-year
workmanship warranty on every residential installation.

**What does the workmanship warranty cover?** Issues caused by installation
errors: panels loosening from improper fastening, posts failing due to
incorrect setting, gates misaligned at install, and similar
installation-related defects. Full terms in the written warranty delivered
with the project.

**What does the warranty NOT cover?** Storm damage, hurricanes, flooding,
vehicle or tree impact, vandalism, lack of maintenance, normal wear and
tear, sun-fade discoloration, or third-party modifications after
installation.

**Is there a manufacturer warranty on materials?** Yes. Vinyl typically
carries a manufacturer limited lifetime warranty. Aluminum typically
carries 10–20 years. Wood and chain-link vary by product.

**Is the warranty transferable?** The workmanship warranty is generally
non-transferable and applies to the original homeowner. Manufacturer
warranties vary.

**Is the warranty provided in writing?** Yes, full details in the written
quote and contract.

**How do I file a warranty claim?** Call (386) 403-9460 or email
info@smoothfenceusa.com with photos and a description. Federico will
schedule an inspection.

**What is your response time?** Short questions, same business day.

**What are your business hours?** Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to
6:00 PM.

**Can I reach you by call or text?** Yes, both.

**What is your contact info?** Phone (386) 403-9460. Email
info@smoothfenceusa.com.

## Services Offered

**What types of fences do you install?** We install vinyl, aluminum,
chain-link, and wood fences.

**Do you offer fence repair?** Yes. We repair damaged sections, leaning
posts, broken panels, gate issues, and some storm-damaged fences.

**Are fences customizable?** Yes. Customized by material, layout, privacy,
and style.

**What's the best material for Florida?** Depends on the project. Vinyl =
low maintenance + privacy. Aluminum = durability + visibility. Chain-link
= cost-effective. Wood = classic look.

**What vinyl styles are available?** Full privacy, privacy with decorative
top, semi-privacy, and picket.

**Is aluminum good for pools?** Yes. Safety, visibility, and clean look.

**Do you install wood fences?** Yes, standard option.

**Can you repair only part of the fence?** Yes when damage is limited.
Widespread structural issues may be better to replace.

**Do you handle storm-damaged fences?** Yes. We inspect and decide repair
vs replacement.

**Do you remove the old fence?** Yes, can be included in the quote when
needed.

**Can you match my existing fence?** Sometimes, depending on material,
style, color, age, and current product availability.

**What gate types and sizes do you offer?** Standard walk-gate sizes are
4 ft and 5 ft wide. We also build custom-size gates to whatever opening
you need, in any material. Custom gates add fabrication, installation,
and transportation charges on top of the standard price.

**Can gates be automated?** No. Smooth Fence USA does not offer gate
automation. All our gates are manually operated. If you need automation,
you can coordinate that separately with an access-control installer after
we complete the gate.

**What hardware do you use?** Heavy-duty galvanized or stainless-steel
hinges and latches rated for the gate's weight and material. We don't
commit to a specific brand — we choose what works best for each project.
If you have a brand preference, we're open to it at your request.

**How do you handle pool gate safety?** Florida pool code typically
requires self-closing and self-latching hardware with the latch at least
54 inches above grade, and gates swinging outward from the pool. We
install pool-code-compliant hardware on request.

**Do you install access control (keypads, card readers)?** We install
standard keyed locks and manual latches only. For keypad, intercom, or
card readers, customers coordinate with a separate access-control
provider.

**Do you set posts in concrete?** Depends on project, soil, local code,
and fence type. Goal is always stable and long-lasting.

**How do fences hold up to Florida weather?** We choose materials and
methods designed for heat, humidity, storms, and coastal conditions.

**What is virgin vinyl?** New, non-recycled vinyl, preferred for
consistency and long-term durability.

**Routed vs bracket-mounted vinyl — what's the difference?** Routed
inserts the rails directly into the posts for a cleaner look.
Bracket-mounted uses external connectors.

**What post caps are available?** Flat, pyramid, gothic, New
England-style, solar-light caps.

## Service Area & Estimates

**What areas do you serve?** Palm Coast, Flagler County, Volusia County,
St. Johns County, Duval County, and Putnam County. Nearby areas may be
possible depending on the address.

**Are estimates free?** Yes. Free, no-obligation on-site estimates.

**What's the fastest way to get a quote?** Use the instant estimator at
smoothfenceusa.com/estimator, then schedule a free on-site visit to
confirm.

**How long does installation take?** Most residential projects are
completed in 1 to 3 days, depending on size, terrain, weather, access,
and material.

**What affects the price of a fence?** Material, height, linear footage,
number of gates, terrain, layout, old fence removal, and any special
site conditions. Firm pricing only after the free on-site visit.

**Do you help with HOA approval?** Yes, we help review HOA requirements
and provide supporting details.

**Who submits the HOA application?** In most cases the homeowner. We
provide the info needed.

**Do projects require permits?** Some projects require city/county
permits, some do not. Depends on location and scope. We guide through
the steps when required.

**How do you handle property lines and easements?** We plan layouts with
them in mind, but final boundary determinations come from a survey or
the proper authority.

**What should I prepare before install day?** Property access clear,
work area cleared, pets secured, any HOA/permit items underway if
needed.
