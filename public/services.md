## What we install and repair

- **Vinyl fence installation** — Low-maintenance vinyl fences that stay
  bright and clean, perfect for privacy and curb appeal in Florida's coastal
  climate. Virgin vinyl. Styles: full privacy, privacy with decorative top,
  semi-privacy, picket. Routed or bracket-mounted.
- **Aluminum fence installation** — Sleek, durable aluminum fences that
  won't rust and are ideal for pools, front yards, and HOA communities.
  Pool-code compliant hardware available on request.
- **Chain-link fence installation** — A cost-effective way to secure your
  property, pets, or work areas without blocking visibility.
- **Wood fence installation** — Classic wood fences with modern hardware
  and proper post setting so they don't lean or sag after the first storm.
- **Fence repair & storm damage** — We fix leaning panels, broken posts,
  and storm-damaged sections so you don't have to replace the whole fence.
- **HOA & permits assistance** — We help you navigate HOA guidelines and
  local permitting so your fence is approved the first time.

## Gates

- Standard walk-gates: 4 ft and 5 ft wide.
- Custom gates: any opening, any material; adds fabrication, installation,
  and transportation charges on top of the standard price.
- Hardware: heavy-duty galvanized or stainless-steel hinges and latches
  sized to the gate's weight and material. No brand commitment (customer
  preferences accepted on request).
- **Gates are manual only.** We do not offer automation, keypads, intercoms,
  or card readers. If you need access control, coordinate with a separate
  installer after we complete the gate.
- Pool gate safety: Florida pool code typically requires self-closing,
  self-latching hardware with the latch ≥54 inches above grade, and gates
  swinging outward from the pool. We install pool-code-compliant hardware
  on request.

## Frequently asked questions

- **What areas do you serve?** Flagler, Volusia, St. Johns, Duval, and
  Putnam counties across Northeast Florida. Contact us to confirm
  availability in your specific area.
- **Do you help with HOA approvals and permits?** Yes, we guide homeowners
  through HOA requirements and local permitting to avoid delays and
  rejections.
- **How long does a typical fence installation take?** Most residential
  fence projects take 1–3 days once materials are onsite and approvals are
  complete.
- **Can you repair part of my fence instead of replacing it?** Often yes.
  We repair damaged panels, leaning posts, and storm damage without
  replacing the entire fence.
- **Which fence material works best in Florida's climate?** Vinyl and
  aluminum handle moisture, heat, and storms very well. Wood can also
  perform great with proper installation.
