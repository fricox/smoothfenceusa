# HOA & permits made simple

Many neighborhoods across Flagler, Volusia, St. Johns and the surrounding
Northeast Florida communities are part of an HOA. That means your new fence
must follow specific rules for height, style, material, and color. On top
of that, some projects require city or county permits.

Smooth Fence USA helps you understand what's allowed before you sign a
contract so you don't waste time or money on plans that won't be approved.

## HOA guidance

- Review of your HOA rules and design guidelines.
- Help choosing fence styles and colors that fit.
- Support with drawings or descriptions for approvals.

## Permits & inspections

- Guidance on what projects require permits.
- Coordination of permit steps when applicable.
- Fence layouts that respect property lines and easements.

## Who submits what?

In most cases the homeowner submits the HOA application — we prepare the
documentation (drawings, materials, color, height) so the application is
complete and approval-friendly. For permits, we guide the steps and
coordinate when applicable; final boundary determinations come from a
survey or the proper authority.

## What we need from you

Every neighborhood is different. When you request a quote, let us know if
you're part of an HOA and we'll factor that into your plan from day one.
